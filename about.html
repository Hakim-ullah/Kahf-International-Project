ABOUT US

For over seventy years, Kahf International is proud to be part of the worldwide network of Official Rolex Retailers, allowed to sell and maintain Rolex watches.

Kahf International is more than a name – it is a legacy; its lineage can be traced back to the year 1948, when the late Mr. Mohammad Saeed: a watchmaker by profession established ‘M Rahim Bux & Co’, the first officially accredited Rolex outlet in Pakistan.

At the heart of KAHF is the perpetual passion for horology and so it is that seventy years on KAHF remains family-operated, its name has become a passion for timekeeping.

Over the years we have introduced numerous watchmaking & jewelry brands, catering to different echelons of Pakistani society. Presently, Kahf International is the official retailer of Rolex and official national distributor for Longines, Continental & EverSwiss watches. These brands are retailed at our own outlets in Islamabad, Karachi & Lahore.
